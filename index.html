Meet Josie Prosdocimo

Josie Prosdocimo

Founder & Principal Consultant

Josie is an experienced leader who has excelled at helping both businesses and individuals turn ideas into outcomes. Known for her practical approach, clarity, and ability to execute, she has guided organizations through complex growth and transformation—always with a focus on people, purpose, and measurable results.

Through LaVita Consulting, Josie helps businesses build the right foundations for their cross-functional teams to thrive, reducing churn, improving collaboration, and accelerating outcomes. Her work blends deep operational experience with a grounded perspective on leadership and growth, rooted in a simple belief: when organizations and individuals align around what truly matters, progress follows organically.
